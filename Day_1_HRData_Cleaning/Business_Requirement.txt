Data Cleaning Task

As a Data Analyst working in Palmoria Manufacturing group, Nigeria.
You've been given a dataset and have been required to perform basic
data cleaning using POWERBI to prepare the data for analysis.

POINTERS FROM STAKEHOLDER
1. Generally, there are two genders in the organization.
However, some employees refused to disclose their gender.
You would need to assign a generic gender status to these employees.

2. Some employees are without salary because they are no longer with the 
company. You will need to take those employees out.

3. Lastly, some department are indicated as NULL. These department also need to be taken
out.
